# Touchpoint 1 Agenda

**Recommended timing:** Early in the tracking period  
**Recommended length:** 30 minutes

## Goals

- Confirm the scout is actually doing and tracking responsibilities.
- Check that parents or guardians understand their supporting role.
- Coach the scout on communication, follow-through, and initiative.

## Agenda

| Time | Topic | Counselor notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 5 min | Tracking status | Look for dates, specific responsibilities, and parent initials or notes. |
| 10 min | Responsibility reflection | Ask what has been easier or harder than expected. |
| 10 min | Family project progress | Confirm the project is useful and appropriately scoped. |
| 5 min | Next steps | Identify one improvement before the next check-in. |

## Coaching prompts

- Which responsibility is easiest to forget?
- What did your family notice without you pointing it out?
- Where did you need reminders, and why?
- How are you showing initiative rather than just compliance?
