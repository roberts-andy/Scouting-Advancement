# Parent and Scout Kickoff Email

**Subject:** Family Life Merit Badge - expectations and parent role

Hi Scouts and parents,

We are starting the Family Life merit badge. This badge works best when the scout practices real responsibility at home, participates in family conversations, and completes a useful family project.

Parents and guardians can help by confirming responsibilities, providing feedback, and helping the scout choose a meaningful project. The scout should still own the work and be ready to explain what they did and learned.

At the kickoff meeting, scouts will:

- Set up a home responsibility tracker.
- Brainstorm a family project.
- Learn what family discussions and reflections they need to prepare.
- Review how check-ins and final review will work.

Please have each scout bring the current official merit badge pamphlet or access to the current official requirements.
