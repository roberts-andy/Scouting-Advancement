# Parent and Scout Kickoff Email

**Subject:** Personal Management Merit Badge - 13-week tracking expectations

Hi Scouts and parents,

We are starting the Personal Management merit badge. This badge works best when scouts treat it as a real-life habit-building project, not a one-time class.

The most important part is the 13-week budget and tracking period. Scouts should track real income, expenses, savings, and spending decisions as they happen for 13 consecutive weeks. Please do not wait until the end and recreate the log from memory.

At the kickoff meeting, scouts will:

- Choose a tracking method, such as the provided spreadsheet, an app, or a notebook.
- Draft an initial budget.
- Start a major purchase and savings plan.
- Learn what they need to bring to check-ins and the final review.

Parents can help by reminding scouts to track consistently, but the scout should own the work and be able to explain their choices.

Please have each scout bring the current official merit badge pamphlet or access to the current official requirements.
