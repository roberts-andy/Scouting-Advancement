# Final Review Meeting Agenda

**Recommended timing:** After week 13 is complete  
**Recommended length:** 45-60 minutes

## Goals

- Review the complete 13-week log for consistency and realism.
- Discuss budget vs. actual results.
- Validate understanding through explanation and reflection.
- Confirm completion only when the scout can explain what they learned.

## Agenda

| Time | Topic | Counselor notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 10 min | Tracking completeness | Confirm 13 consecutive weeks and look for realistic entries. |
| 15 min | Budget vs. actual review | Ask the scout to explain variances and what they mean. |
| 10 min | Major purchase plan | Discuss pricing, savings, tradeoffs, and whether the plan changed. |
| 15 min | Financial literacy discussion | Use the question bank to probe understanding. |
| 5 min | Final reflection | Ask what habit or decision-making skill will stick. |

## Key final questions

- Where did your plan break?
- What would you change next time?
- Which spending category surprised you most?
- What habit did you build?
- How will you manage money differently after this badge?
