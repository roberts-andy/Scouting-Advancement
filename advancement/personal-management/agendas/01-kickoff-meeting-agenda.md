# Kickoff Meeting Agenda

**Recommended length:** 60-90 minutes

## Goals

- Explain that this badge is built around real 13-week behavior, not a worksheet sprint.
- Start the major purchase savings plan.
- Set up each scout's budget and tracking method.
- Begin discussion-heavy financial literacy topics.

## Agenda

| Time | Topic | Counselor notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 10 min | What personal management means | Frame this as habits, choices, tradeoffs, and reflection. |
| 15 min | Budget basics | Explain income, expenses, savings, giving, planned vs. actual, and why tracking matters. |
| 20 min | Major purchase workshop | Scouts choose a meaningful item, estimate cost, compare options, and identify tradeoffs. |
| 20 min | 13-week budget setup | Scouts choose a tracking method and draft categories. Emphasize real-time tracking. |
| 20 min | Guided discussion topics | Start conversations on needs vs. wants, buyer's remorse, credit/debit/cash, saving, and investing. |
| 5 min | Close and expectations | Confirm start date, first check-in date, and no backfilling expectation. |

## Scout leaves with

- A selected 13-week tracking start date.
- A draft budget with realistic categories.
- A major purchase idea and initial savings plan.
- Clear instructions to track real income and expenses as they happen.

## Closing script

For the next 13 consecutive weeks, track real money as it happens. Do not recreate the log from memory at the end. The goal is not perfect spending; the goal is honest tracking and thoughtful reflection.
