# Final Review Checklist

Use this as a counselor-facing checklist during the final meeting.

## Materials reviewed

- [ ] Scout used the current official requirements as the source of truth.
- [ ] 13 consecutive weeks are represented.
- [ ] Income, expenses, savings, and relevant notes are tracked.
- [ ] Budget vs. actual comparison is complete.
- [ ] Major purchase and savings plan is complete enough for discussion.
- [ ] Scout is prepared to discuss required financial literacy topics.

## Quality review

- [ ] Entries appear realistic and timely.
- [ ] Categories make sense for the scout's life.
- [ ] Scout can explain at least two budget variances.
- [ ] Scout can describe a spending tradeoff.
- [ ] Scout can explain what they would change next time.
- [ ] Scout can describe one habit they want to keep.

## Counselor notes

Record observations separately if needed. Focus on understanding, honesty, and reflection rather than perfect financial behavior.
