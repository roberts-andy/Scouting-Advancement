# Discussion Question Bank

Use these questions to help scouts explain their thinking. Pick the questions that fit the scout's age, maturity, and real examples.

## Budgeting and tracking

- What was the hardest transaction type to remember to track?
- Which category was most different from your original budget?
- Did tracking change your behavior while the 13 weeks were still happening?
- What purchase felt small at the time but added up over the full period?
- If your budget was wrong, what assumption caused the miss?

## Needs, wants, and tradeoffs

- What is one purchase you wanted but decided not to make?
- When does a want become important enough to plan for?
- What would you give up to afford your major purchase sooner?
- What is the difference between cheap and good value?

## Buyer behavior

- Have you ever regretted a purchase? What made it a bad decision?
- How do ads, friends, games, apps, or social media influence spending?
- Is it easier to spend with cash, card, online checkout, or a phone? Why?
- What rule could help you pause before an impulse purchase?

## Saving and investing

- Why might someone keep some money in savings instead of investing everything?
- Why might someone invest money they do not need soon?
- What does risk mean in investing?
- Why is time important for compound growth?
- Why is diversification safer than putting everything into one thing?

## Credit, debt, and responsibility

- Why can borrowing money be useful?
- Why can borrowing money be dangerous?
- What happens if someone only thinks about the monthly payment and not the total cost?
- How could a credit card help or hurt someone?

## Insurance and risk

- Why do people pay for insurance if they hope never to use it?
- What kinds of events could create costs too large for a normal budget?
- How do deductibles and premiums trade off against each other?

## Final reflection

- Where did your plan break?
- What would you change next time?
- What habit did you build?
- What did you learn about yourself?
- How will you manage money differently after this badge?
