# Personal Management Merit Badge Counselor Kit

This folder contains a reusable counseling kit for running Personal Management as a habit-building badge rather than a one-time class.

## Recommended flow

1. **Kickoff meeting** - Set expectations, start the major purchase plan, launch the 13-week budget/tracking period, and begin discussion topics.
2. **Touchpoint 1** - Review early tracking quality, category choices, and whether the budget reflects reality.
3. **Touchpoint 2** - Prepare scouts for final analysis: budget vs. actual, adjustments, and learning.
4. **Final meeting** - Review the full 13-week log, confirm understanding through discussion, and capture reflection.

## Important counseling note

Use the current official merit badge pamphlet and official requirements as the source of truth. These materials are designed to help you coach, track, and discuss the work; they intentionally do not reproduce the official requirements verbatim.
