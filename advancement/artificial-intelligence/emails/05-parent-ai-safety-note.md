# Parent AI Safety Note

**Subject:** Artificial Intelligence Merit Badge - privacy and safety reminder

Hi parents,

As scouts work on the Artificial Intelligence merit badge, please remind them not to enter private, sensitive, or identifying information into public AI tools. This includes home addresses, school account information, medical details, private family information, passwords, and other people's personal information.

Helpful parent support includes:

- Discussing family expectations for AI use.
- Helping scouts identify trustworthy sources for fact-checking.
- Asking what the scout learned about AI limitations.
- Encouraging the scout to do their own thinking and writing.

The goal is not to become an AI expert overnight. The goal is to become a thoughtful, safe, and responsible technology user.
