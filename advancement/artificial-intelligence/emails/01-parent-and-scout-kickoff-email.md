# Parent and Scout Kickoff Email

**Subject:** Artificial Intelligence Merit Badge - kickoff and expectations

Hi Scouts and parents,

We are starting the Artificial Intelligence merit badge. This badge works best when scouts explore AI thoughtfully: what it can do, where it fails, how to verify output, and how to use AI responsibly.

At kickoff, scouts will:

- Set up an AI activity log.
- Choose an AI tool, example, or scenario to investigate.
- Discuss privacy, safety, bias, accuracy, and human judgment.
- Learn what evidence to bring to check-ins and final review.

Parents can help by discussing family rules for technology use, reminding scouts not to enter private information into public AI tools, and helping them find trustworthy sources for fact-checking. The scout should own the research, notes, testing, and final explanation.

Please have each scout bring the current official merit badge pamphlet or access to the current official requirements.
