# Final Review Meeting Agenda

**Recommended length:** 45-60 minutes

## Goals

- Review the scout's activity log, research notes, demonstration/project evidence, and final reflection.
- Validate understanding through explanation rather than memorized definitions.
- Confirm the scout can describe AI benefits, limitations, risks, and responsible-use habits.

## Agenda

| Time | Topic | Counselor notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 10 min | Activity log review | Look for prompts, observations, sources, and honest evaluation. |
| 10 min | Demonstration or project | Ask the scout to show or explain what they tested or built. |
| 15 min | Ethics and safety discussion | Discuss privacy, bias, accountability, misinformation, and human judgment. |
| 10 min | Real-world impact | Ask how AI affects school, work, community, accessibility, safety, or careers. |
| 5 min | Final reflection | Ask what habit the scout will keep when using AI tools. |

## Key final questions

- What does AI do that feels impressive?
- What does AI do that people should be cautious about?
- How did you verify an AI output?
- What private or sensitive information should never go into a public AI tool?
- What did this badge change about how you will use technology?
