# Touchpoint 1 Agenda

**Recommended timing:** After initial research and first tool observations  
**Recommended length:** 30 minutes

## Goals

- Confirm scouts are keeping useful notes.
- Check that scouts are verifying AI outputs instead of accepting them at face value.
- Coach scouts on bias, privacy, safety, and evidence quality.

## Agenda

| Time | Topic | Counselor notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 5 min | Status check | Ask what the scout has researched, tried, or observed. |
| 10 min | AI activity log review | Look for prompts, outputs, what changed, and what was verified. |
| 10 min | Reliability and bias discussion | Ask where the AI was useful, wrong, vague, or unfair. |
| 5 min | Next steps | Identify one additional test or source before Touchpoint 2. |

## Coaching prompts

- What did the AI do well?
- What did it get wrong or leave out?
- How did you check whether the output was accurate?
- What information should you avoid sharing with an AI tool?
- Who might be helped or harmed by this use of AI?
