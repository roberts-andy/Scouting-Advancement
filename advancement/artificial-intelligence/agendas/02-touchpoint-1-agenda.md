# Optional Support Check-in Agenda

**Recommended timing:** Only if scouts need help between kickoff and final review  
**Recommended length:** 20-30 minutes

## Purpose

This is optional. Use it when scouts need coaching on their AI activity log, project idea, tool comparison, or verification process. Most scouts should be able to complete the badge with a kickoff/work session and a final review.

## Agenda

| Time | Topic | Counselor notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 5 min | Status check | Ask what the scout has researched, tried, or observed. |
| 10 min | Activity log review | Look for prompts, outputs, what changed, and what was verified. |
| 10 min | Reliability and bias coaching | Ask where the AI was useful, wrong, vague, or unfair. |
| 5 min | Next action | Identify one concrete thing to finish before final review. |

## Coaching prompts

- What did the AI do well?
- What did it get wrong or leave out?
- How did you check whether the output was accurate?
- What information should you avoid sharing with an AI tool?
- Who might be helped or harmed by this use of AI?
