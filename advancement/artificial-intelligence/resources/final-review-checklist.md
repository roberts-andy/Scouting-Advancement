# Final Review Checklist

Use this as a counselor-facing checklist during final review.

## Materials reviewed

- [ ] Scout used the current official requirements as the source of truth.
- [ ] AI activity log is complete enough for discussion.
- [ ] Research notes include sources.
- [ ] Demonstration, comparison, or project evidence is available.
- [ ] Scout prepared notes on safety, privacy, bias, accuracy, and human judgment.

## Quality review

- [ ] Scout can explain AI concepts in their own words.
- [ ] Scout can describe what they tested or observed.
- [ ] Scout can identify where AI output was useful.
- [ ] Scout can identify where AI output was limited, wrong, vague, or risky.
- [ ] Scout can explain how they verified information.
- [ ] Scout can describe one responsible-use habit they will keep.

## Counselor notes

Do not evaluate the scout based on how advanced the tool is. Evaluate whether the scout understands, questions, verifies, and reflects.
