# Artificial Intelligence Traceability Matrix

This matrix explains how the kit materials map to the official badge scope without reproducing official requirement text. Use the current official requirements as the source of truth.

| Official badge area | Kit materials that support it | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| AI and automation fundamentals | Kickoff agenda, workbook `Research Notes`, discussion question bank | Supports key terms and basic understanding in the scout's own words. |
| Real-world AI examples in daily life, school, and work | Workbook `AI Activity Log`, `Tool Evaluation`, kickoff agenda | Helps scouts observe practical examples rather than only discussing theory. |
| Bias, privacy, ethics, and responsible use | Workbook `Safety Ethics Reflection`, parent AI safety note, counselor guide, question bank | Directly supports ethical concerns and safe-use habits. |
| Deepfakes, misinformation, and generated media risk | Discussion question bank, safety/ethics reflection, final review agenda | Prompts scouts to consider misuse and verification. |
| Communicating effectively with AI | Workbook `AI Activity Log`, `ai-activity-log-template.csv`, optional support check-in | Tracks prompts/inputs, outputs, what changed, and how the scout evaluated results. |
| Project, comparison, or teaching activity | Optional project lab agenda, final review agenda, workbook `Tool Evaluation` and `Final Review` | Supports applying knowledge through a concrete demonstration or teaching activity. |
| Career exploration or professional interview/research | Workbook `Research Notes`, `Safety Ethics Reflection`, final review agenda, final review checklist | Ensures the career component is visible in preparation and final discussion. |
