# Artificial Intelligence Merit Badge Counselor Kit

This folder contains a reusable counseling kit for the Artificial Intelligence merit badge. The structure is designed around understanding AI concepts, using AI tools responsibly, examining real-world impacts, practicing prompt-and-evaluate habits, and reflecting on safety, ethics, bias, and human judgment.

## Recommended flow

1. **Kickoff meeting** - Set expectations, introduce AI vocabulary, discuss responsible use, choose research/example areas, and start the AI activity log.
2. **Touchpoint 1** - Review early research, tool observations, safety notes, and whether scouts are distinguishing AI output from verified facts.
3. **Touchpoint 2** - Review project or demonstration progress, ethical tradeoffs, bias testing, and final discussion preparation.
4. **Final meeting** - Review the scout's AI log, research notes, demonstration/project evidence, safety reflection, and ability to explain what they learned.

## Important counseling note

Use the current official merit badge pamphlet and official requirements as the source of truth. These materials help organize coaching and discussion; they intentionally do not reproduce the official requirements verbatim.
