# Discussion Question Bank

## Community understanding

- How did you decide what your community is?
- What services does your community depend on?
- What is one strength of your community?
- What is one challenge people are trying to solve?

## Local issues

- Who is affected by this issue?
- What are two different viewpoints?
- What facts changed your thinking?
- What tradeoffs make this issue difficult?

## Civic participation

- Who had a voice in the meeting or public discussion?
- What did people agree or disagree about?
- How were decisions made or deferred?
- What question would you ask if you could speak?

## Service and organizations

- What need did your service address?
- How did the organization help the community?
- What did you learn from doing service instead of just reading about the issue?
- What would make the service more effective?

## Final reflection

- What does good citizenship look like in your community?
- What action could you keep doing after the badge?
- How should a citizen respond when they disagree with others?
- What did this badge change about how you see your community?
