# Final Review Meeting Agenda

**Recommended length:** 45-60 minutes

## Goals

- Review community research, issue analysis, civic observation, service log, organization research, and final reflection.
- Validate that the scout understands citizenship as action and responsibility.

## Agenda

| Time | Topic | Counselor notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 10 min | Community profile | Ask the scout to describe their community and its needs. |
| 10 min | Issue research | Discuss facts, stakeholders, and differing viewpoints. |
| 10 min | Civic observation | Ask what the scout learned about public decision-making. |
| 10 min | Service and organization | Review service hours and how the work helped. |
| 10 min | Final reflection | Ask how the scout will continue being a citizen in the community. |

## Key final questions

- What does good citizenship look like where you live?
- What issue did you understand better after research?
- What did service teach you?
- What is one action you could keep doing?
