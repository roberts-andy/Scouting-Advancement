# Touchpoint 1 Agenda

**Recommended timing:** After initial research  
**Recommended length:** 30 minutes

## Goals

- Review community profile and local issue research.
- Confirm the scout is using credible sources and noticing different viewpoints.
- Check the plan for civic meeting or public discussion observation.

## Agenda

| Time | Topic | Counselor notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 10 min | Community profile | Ask what surprised the scout about their community. |
| 10 min | Issue research | Push for facts, affected groups, and multiple viewpoints. |
| 5 min | Meeting plan | Confirm what the scout will observe and how they will take notes. |
| 5 min | Next steps | Identify service and organization research actions. |

## Coaching prompts

- Who is affected by this issue?
- What are two different ways people might view it?
- What source do you trust most, and why?
- What question will you listen for at the meeting?
