# Citizenship in the Community Merit Badge Counselor Kit

This folder contains a reusable counseling kit for Citizenship in the Community. The structure is designed around understanding a real community, researching local issues, observing civic participation, serving others, and reflecting on what citizenship means in practice.

## Recommended flow

1. **Kickoff meeting** - Define community, launch research, discuss civic participation, and choose service planning next steps.
2. **Touchpoint 1** - Review community profile, issue research, and meeting/observation plan.
3. **Touchpoint 2** - Review service plan, organization research, and reflection preparation.
4. **Final meeting** - Discuss community issue, civic observation, service experience, media/story reflection, and future action.

## Important counseling note

Use the current official merit badge pamphlet and official requirements as the source of truth. These materials help organize coaching and discussion; they intentionally do not reproduce official requirements verbatim.
