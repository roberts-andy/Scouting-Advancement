# Final Review Meeting Agenda

**Recommended length:** 45-60 minutes

## Goals

- Review home responsibility tracking, family meeting notes, project outcomes, and final reflection.
- Validate that the scout understands family roles, communication, and contribution.
- Confirm completion through explanation, not paperwork alone.

## Agenda

| Time | Topic | Counselor notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 10 min | Tracking review | Look for consistency and honest notes. |
| 10 min | Family project | Ask what problem the project solved and how the scout led or contributed. |
| 15 min | Family discussion | Explore communication, decision-making, and responsibilities. |
| 10 min | Reflection | Ask what habit the scout wants to keep. |

## Key final questions

- How did your family depend on you during this badge?
- What was harder than expected?
- What did you learn about communication at home?
- What would you do differently next time?
