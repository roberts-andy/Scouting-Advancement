# Touchpoint 2 Agenda

**Recommended timing:** Mid-to-late badge work  
**Recommended length:** 30 minutes

## Goals

- Review family project execution and family meeting notes.
- Prepare the scout for final discussion and reflection.
- Identify missing evidence before the final meeting.

## Agenda

| Time | Topic | Counselor notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 5 min | Status check | Confirm what is complete, in progress, or blocked. |
| 10 min | Project review | Discuss planning, family benefit, and scout ownership. |
| 10 min | Family discussions | Ask what the scout heard, contributed, and learned. |
| 5 min | Final prep | Assign final reflection prompts. |

## Coaching prompts

- What did you learn about how your family makes decisions?
- What part of the project required the most planning?
- What would your family say changed in your contribution at home?
- What do you still need to ask or discuss before final review?
