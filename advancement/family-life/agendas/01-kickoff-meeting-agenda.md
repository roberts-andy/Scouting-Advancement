# Kickoff Meeting Agenda

**Recommended length:** 60 minutes

## Goals

- Explain that this badge is about participation in family life, not just talking about family life.
- Review expectations for home responsibility tracking, family conversations, and the family project.
- Help each scout choose realistic responsibilities and identify a project that genuinely helps the household.

## Agenda

| Time | Topic | Counselor notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 10 min | What Family Life is about | Frame the badge around reliability, communication, service at home, and reflection. |
| 15 min | Home responsibilities | Scouts pick responsibilities that are meaningful, visible, and trackable. |
| 15 min | Family project brainstorm | Encourage projects that solve a real household need, not a token task. |
| 15 min | Family discussion setup | Explain how scouts should prepare for and participate in family conversations. |
| 5 min | Close and next steps | Confirm tracking start date, parent role, and first touchpoint. |

## Scout leaves with

- A tracking method started.
- A list of responsibilities to discuss with parents or guardians.
- At least two possible family project ideas.
- Clear understanding that they must be ready to explain what they learned.
