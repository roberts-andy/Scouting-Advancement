# Family Life Merit Badge Counselor Kit

This folder contains a reusable counseling kit for Family Life. The structure is designed around follow-through at home, family conversation, a family project, and reflection.

## Recommended flow

1. **Kickoff meeting** - Set expectations, review the current official requirements, launch home responsibility tracking, and brainstorm family project ideas.
2. **Touchpoint 1** - Review early consistency, confirm family involvement, and coach the scout on communication.
3. **Touchpoint 2** - Review project progress, family meeting notes, and discussion preparation.
4. **Final meeting** - Review completed tracking, project outcomes, family discussions, and scout reflection.

## Important counseling note

Use the current official merit badge pamphlet and official requirements as the source of truth. These materials help organize coaching and discussion; they intentionally do not reproduce official requirements verbatim.
