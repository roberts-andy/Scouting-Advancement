# Scout Instructions

## Your goal

Practice responsibility at home, participate in family conversations, complete a useful family project, and reflect on what you learned.

## What to track

- Responsibilities you complete.
- Dates and notes.
- Family meeting or discussion topics.
- Project tasks and outcomes.
- Reflection notes for final review.

## What to bring

| Meeting | Bring |
|---|---|
| Kickoff | Current official requirements and family project ideas. |
| Touchpoint 1 | Responsibility tracker and early project notes. |
| Touchpoint 2 | Project plan, family discussion notes, and tracker. |
| Final review | Completed tracker, project outcome, discussion notes, and reflections. |
