# Discussion Question Bank

## Responsibility and follow-through

- What responsibility mattered most to your family?
- When did you need reminders, and what did that teach you?
- What does dependability look like at home?
- How did your attitude affect the work?

## Communication

- What makes family communication hard?
- How does listening change a disagreement?
- What did you learn from a family member's point of view?
- How should families make decisions when people disagree?

## Family project

- What problem did your project solve?
- How did you plan before acting?
- What changed between your plan and the actual work?
- How did your family benefit?

## Final reflection

- What habit do you want to keep?
- What would your family say you improved?
- What would you do differently next time?
- How did this badge change how you see your role at home?
