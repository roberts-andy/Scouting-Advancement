# Family Life Traceability Matrix

This matrix explains how the kit materials map to the official badge scope without reproducing official requirement text. Use the current official requirements as the source of truth.

| Official badge area | Kit materials that support it | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Home responsibilities over time | Workbook `Home Responsibility Tracker`, `home-responsibility-log-template.csv`, touchpoint 1 agenda, parent nudge | Supports repeated follow-through and family accountability. |
| Family project planning and completion | Workbook `Project Planner`, `family-project-plan-template.csv`, kickoff agenda, touchpoint 2 agenda, final review agenda | Helps scouts plan, execute, and explain a project that benefits the family. |
| Family meetings and family participation | Workbook `Family Meeting Log`, kickoff agenda, touchpoint agendas, final review email | Supports family involvement and discussion evidence. |
| Family communication and decision-making | `resources/discussion-question-bank.md`, `resources/counselor-guide.md`, final review agenda | Prompts scouts to explain listening, conflict, roles, and decision-making. |
| Sensitive family discussion topics | `resources/counselor-guide.md`, `resources/discussion-question-bank.md`, final review checklist | Counselor should use discretion and current official requirements; do not force public disclosure of private family details. |
| Effective parenting / future family roles reflection | `resources/discussion-question-bank.md`, workbook `Final Reflection`, final review agenda | Supports reflection on what makes families healthy and what role the scout wants to play. |
| Parent or guardian support | Kickoff email, parent progress nudge | Clarifies that families support the work while the scout owns tracking and reflection. |
