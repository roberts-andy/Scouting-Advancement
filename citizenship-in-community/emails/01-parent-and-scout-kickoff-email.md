# Parent and Scout Kickoff Email

**Subject:** Citizenship in the Community Merit Badge - kickoff and expectations

Hi Scouts and parents,

We are starting the Citizenship in the Community merit badge. This badge works best when scouts research their own community, observe how people participate in public life, complete meaningful service, and reflect on what good citizenship looks like.

Parents can help with transportation, safety, and identifying appropriate meetings or service opportunities. The scout should own the research, note-taking, service reflection, and final explanation.

At kickoff, scouts will:

- Define the community they will study.
- Start a community profile.
- Choose a local issue to research.
- Plan civic observation and service next steps.

Please have each scout bring the current official merit badge pamphlet or access to the current official requirements.
