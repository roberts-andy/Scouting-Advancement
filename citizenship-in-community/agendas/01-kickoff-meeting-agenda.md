# Kickoff Meeting Agenda

**Recommended length:** 60-75 minutes

## Goals

- Help scouts define their community in a practical way.
- Explain that citizenship is active participation, not just facts about government.
- Launch community profile, issue research, civic meeting observation, and service planning.

## Agenda

| Time | Topic | Counselor notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 10 min | What is a community? | Include place, people, institutions, needs, and shared responsibility. |
| 15 min | Community profile | Scouts identify services, leaders, strengths, and challenges. |
| 15 min | Local issue research | Scouts choose a real issue and identify multiple viewpoints. |
| 15 min | Civic observation and service planning | Discuss meetings, organizations, and service options. |
| 10 min | Close and expectations | Confirm next research steps and touchpoint date. |

## Scout leaves with

- A community to research.
- A short list of possible local issues.
- A plan to observe civic participation.
- Service organization ideas to investigate.
