# Touchpoint 2 Agenda

**Recommended timing:** After civic observation or service planning  
**Recommended length:** 30 minutes

## Goals

- Review meeting notes and what the scout learned about civic participation.
- Confirm service plan and organization research.
- Prepare final reflection around active citizenship.

## Agenda

| Time | Topic | Counselor notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 10 min | Civic observation | Ask what decisions, tradeoffs, or concerns were discussed. |
| 10 min | Service planning | Confirm the service activity is useful and properly coordinated. |
| 5 min | Organization research | Ask how the organization improves the community. |
| 5 min | Final prep | Assign final discussion prompts. |

## Coaching prompts

- What did you notice about how people speak up in public settings?
- How does the organization you chose address a real need?
- What did service teach you that research alone did not?
- What civic action could you keep doing after the badge?
