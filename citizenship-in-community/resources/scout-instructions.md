# Scout Instructions

## Your goal

Learn how your community works, study a real issue, observe civic participation, serve others, and explain what good citizenship means in action.

## What to track

- Community research notes and sources.
- Local issue facts and viewpoints.
- Civic meeting or public discussion notes.
- Service dates, hours, and reflections.
- Organization research.
- Final discussion notes.

## What to bring

| Meeting | Bring |
|---|---|
| Kickoff | Current official requirements and ideas about your community. |
| Touchpoint 1 | Community profile, issue research, and civic observation plan. |
| Touchpoint 2 | Meeting notes, service plan/log, and organization research. |
| Final review | Completed research, service log, and final reflection notes. |
