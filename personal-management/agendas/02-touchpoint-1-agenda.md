# Touchpoint 1 Agenda

**Recommended timing:** Week 4 or 5  
**Recommended length:** 30 minutes

## Goals

- Confirm scouts are tracking consistently.
- Catch unrealistic categories or missing transactions early.
- Coach scouts on what their early data is showing.

## Agenda

| Time | Topic | Counselor notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 5 min | Status check | Ask each scout whether they have tracked every week so far. |
| 10 min | Tracking review | Look for dates, categories, realistic amounts, and notes. |
| 10 min | Budget vs. reality | Ask where actual spending is already different from the plan. |
| 5 min | Next steps | Have scouts adjust categories if needed and continue tracking. |

## Coaching prompts

- What surprised you about your spending so far?
- Which category is hardest to estimate?
- Are you missing small purchases because they seem unimportant?
- If your budget is off, is the issue the plan or the behavior?

## Watch-outs

- Round numbers everywhere.
- Identical spending every week.
- No small transactions.
- Scouts who say they will fill it in later.
