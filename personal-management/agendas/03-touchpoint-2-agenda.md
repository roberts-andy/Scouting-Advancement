# Touchpoint 2 Agenda

**Recommended timing:** Week 9 or 10  
**Recommended length:** 30 minutes

## Goals

- Prepare scouts for the final analysis and reflection.
- Reinforce the difference between tracking data and learning from data.
- Identify gaps before the 13-week period ends.

## Agenda

| Time | Topic | Counselor notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 5 min | Tracking status | Confirm weeks completed and remaining. |
| 10 min | Budget vs. actual preview | Review patterns, surprises, and changes in categories. |
| 10 min | Major purchase progress | Revisit the savings plan and tradeoffs. |
| 5 min | Final meeting prep | Explain what scouts should be ready to discuss after week 13. |

## Coaching prompts

- What pattern do you see now that you did not see in week 1?
- What would you change if you restarted the 13-week period today?
- How did using cash, debit, online purchases, or parent-paid items affect your tracking?
- What tradeoff are you making for your major purchase?
